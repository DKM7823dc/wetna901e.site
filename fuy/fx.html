Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Return Window

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be returned within this timeframe to be eligible for a refund or exchange.

2. Eligible Items

To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Be unused and in the same condition as received
  • Include original tags and packaging
  • Be accompanied by proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
  • Not be personalized or custom-made items

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Personalized or engraved items
  • Items that show signs of wear or damage
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

4. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team at [email protected]
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive a return authorization (RA) number and shipping instructions
  4. Pack the item securely with all original materials
  5. Ship the item to the address provided (you are responsible for return shipping costs)

5. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable (unless the item is defective)

6. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for size or color variations of the same product, subject to availability. Exchange requests must be made within the 30-day return window. Contact customer service to arrange an exchange.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We will provide a prepaid return label at no cost to you
  • You will receive a full refund or replacement, including original shipping costs

8. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless:

  • The item received was incorrect
  • The item is damaged or defective
  • We made an error in fulfilling your order

We recommend using a trackable shipping service for returns. We are not responsible for items lost in transit.

9. International Returns

International customers are responsible for all return shipping costs and any customs fees or duties. Returns from international locations may take longer to process.
